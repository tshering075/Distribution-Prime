This Privacy Policy describes how the Distribution Prime web application (“App,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the App. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

The App is operated by Distribution Prime. For privacy questions, contact: codewynbuild@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

  • To provide, operate, and secure the App.
  • To authenticate users and enforce access controls.

  • To send transactional or operational emails when you use email features (including via Gmail API when connected).
  • To comply with law or protect rights and safety where required.

4. Google API Services — Gmail (Limited Use)

The App’s use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we only use Gmail data to provide or improve user-facing features of the App that are prominent in the App’s interface (for example, sending order emails and processing reply content to update order status). We do not sell Gmail data. We do not use Gmail data for serving advertisements. We do not allow humans to read Gmail content except (a) with your consent, (b) for security or compliance, (c) when required by law, or (d) when aggregated and anonymized for internal operations.
